Maintenance Technician

2016-2017

Hollywood Bowl

  • Manage all planned maintenance and servicing of lanes and equipment.
  • Carry out inspections and reconditioning of equipment.
  • Monitor and improve processes.
  • Provide weekly and monthly reports on performance to senior managers.
  • Monitor and report any repair trends to senior managers.
  • Test and fault-find finished systems in accordance with company procedures.
  • Make sure all safety precautions are taken.
  • Carry out safety tests and record results on mechanical equipment and systems and recommend improvements
  • Responsible for the plant and equipment maintenance and replacement of worn-out parts
  • Scrutinise technical mechanical problems and follow corrective procedures to fix the problem and avoid recurrence.
  • Explain technical problems to non-technical colleagues.
  • Responsible for coordinating and planning daily maintenance and repair activities.
